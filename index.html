Raiden Shogun

Raiden Shogun is a character from Genshin Impact, a 2020 action role-playing gacha game developed by miHoYo. Added in a 2021 update for the game, she was initially a boss enemy for players to fight, and then later released as a playable character. Within the game's story, she is the ruling Archon of the game's Inazuma region. Consisting of two separate beings, the puppet body "Shogun" and the consciousness of Archon Raiden Ei, she originally acts as an authoritarian and stern ruler.

Yae Miko

Yae Miko[Note 1] (Japanese: 八や重え神み子こ Yae Miko), also known as Guuji Yae (Japanese: 宮ぐう司じ Guuji) or the Guuji, is a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact. A kitsune of many facets, Yae Miko oversees the Grand Narukami Shrine, owns the Yae Publishing House, and is Eternity's servant and friend
